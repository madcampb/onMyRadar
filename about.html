Interesting talks, events, and shows collated by Jess Chia.

Doesn’t this already exist? Absolutely. This is more or less just the stuff I’m interested in and will be up to. I’ll probably leave out the more obvious things (exhibitions at the major museums, for example) — my hope is that this will stay fairly brief to avoid a bloated list that requires a lot of sifting through.

Why am I doing this? I go to a lot of things alone, and honestly, I’m still on the fence about whether I prefer to keep it that way LOLLL. Sometimes, though, I do think it would be fun to experience things with other people and be able to process ideas together.

Other times, I’ll go to something and think: More people should be here, experiencing this knowledge/wisdom/beauty!

And lastly, seeing the rate of change in New York City makes me sad sometimes. While it’s easy to feel like the city is just one giant, snaking line outside a Crumbl, engaging with the city in this way is partly what keeps my love for this place alive and my cynicism in check.
