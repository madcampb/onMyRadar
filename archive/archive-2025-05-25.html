MAY 25, 2025: my heart skipped a beat

 

TALKS:

150th Anniversary Exhibition Opening

Art Students League

The Art Students League is celebrating its 150th anniversary — or sesquicentennial, as I’ve just learned. Attend the New York Historical Society’s (kinda pricey) talk or go to the ASL’s exhibition opening on Thursday eve (show will be on til August 17). 

 

 

FILM:

 

Starting Friday, May 30

Before Sunrise

IFC 

323 5th Ave 

Numerous showtimes ($18 + fees, tickets here)

 

*Starting June 6 -  Before Sunset

*Starting June 13 -  Before Midnight

 

Have I seen these movies at Ieast 5-10 times each? Can I stream them online and play my decades-old dvds? Yes, yes, and ye…well, I don’t actually have a dvd drive anymore. Regardless, my heart skipped when I saw the IFC is screening all three. I first watched “Before Sunrise” in high school, and I don’t care if this makes me a huge romantic cheeseball but I will stan these movies for the rest of my life!!!!!!  These days, “Before Midnight” is the one that takes the cake for me (differing opinions welcome).

 

If you don’t know what I’m talking about, please for the love of god go to all three, even if you will never experience the exquisite pain and ecstasy of waiting nine years to find out if baby missed that plane!!!!!!!!
