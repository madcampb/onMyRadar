Tuesday, May 13

A presentation by the Victorian Society New York’s 2025 Emerging Scholars winners

The Center at West Park

165 West 86th St

6:30 - 8pm (pay as you wish donation - tickets here)

 

The Victorian Society New York’s 2025 Emerging Scholars winners will shed light on little-known yet influential aspects of Gilded Age history, literature, international trade, and design. [Guest submission by dear friend and woman about town, NG]

 

Wednesday, May 14

Chris “Daze” Ellis and Charlie Ahearn on Martin Wong’s Legacy and the 80s downtown art scene

Museum of the City of New York

1220 5th Ave

6:30 - 8pm ($15 general admission - tickets here)

 

Martin Wong is a name that has come up repeatedly for me over the past six months, so I’m interested in learning more (the accompanying exhibition is centered on the collection of graffiti he donated to the museum, though, and not his own paintings). I have also been meaning to watch Ahearn’s 1983 documentary, “Wild Style” for some time now, and recently discovered the whole thing is up on YouTube!
